HOW TO PLAY

Tap the screen to thrust upward. Fly through the gaps, grab the garbage, survive all 5 levels.

Ships (more risk = more score)

  • Scout — small & agile · ×1.00
  • Hauler — balanced · ×1.25
  • Freighter — big & heavy · ×1.60

Campaign

  1. Sunset — 0 … 49
  2. City — 50 … 109
  3. Volcano — 110 … 179
  4. Trench — 180 … 239 (anglerfish)
  5. Neon City — 240 … 299 (cyberdragon)
  6. Canyon — 300 … 389 (pirate ambush)
  7. Swamp — 390 … 469 (boss tentacles)
  8. Ice Cave — 470 + (frost wraith)

Power-ups

  • ◎ Shield — absorbs a hit (stacks to 3)
  • ◐ Slow — pipes at ½ speed · 5s
  • ×2 Double — score ×2 · 6s
  • ✦ Magnet — pulls pickups · 6s
  • ◌ Ghost — phase through · 3s
  • ➤ Missile — homes to next pillar · also hits the pirate on canyon

Scoring

  • Clear a pillar: +1 · collect garbage: +1
  • Glowing gold items: +5
  • Missile kills: +1 pillar, +5 pirate
  • Chain pickups for ×2 / ×3 / ×4 combo bonus
